The Tropical Rainforest

A brief summary...

The tropical rainforest is one of the most interesting habitats in the world. It's filled with thousands of species including mammals, reptiles, and birds. Tropical rainforests are located near the equator which is why they're as warm as they are. The animals living in the tropics are a prize to see as no two are the same. And their bold prints and vibrancy could steal a runway show.
